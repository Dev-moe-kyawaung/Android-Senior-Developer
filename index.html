Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my work and services

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in Android development with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, MVVM/Clean Architecture, Firebase, REST APIs, and CI/CD pipelines. I also have experience with AI/ML integration, ethical hacking, and modern web technologies.

How many years of experience do you have?

I have nearly 12 years of hands-on experience in Android development, starting from Java and evolving to modern Kotlin with Jetpack Compose. I've built over 3,000 applications and managed 122+ GitHub repositories.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

I work both remotely and on-site. I'm based between Tachileik, Myanmar and Bangkok, Thailand, but I'm open to remote opportunities worldwide and willing to relocate for the right position.

What is your approach to app architecture?

I follow Clean Architecture principles with MVVM/MVI patterns. I emphasize separation of concerns, testability, and maintainability. My code follows SOLID principles and uses dependency injection (Hilt/Dagger) for better modularity.

Can you help with existing projects?

Absolutely! I can help with bug fixes, feature additions, code refactoring, performance optimization, migration to modern tech stacks, and adding new integrations to existing Android projects.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, I provide comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, updates, performance monitoring, crash analysis, and feature enhancements. I believe in long-term partnerships with my clients.

What's your typical project timeline?

Timeline varies by project scope. A simple app might take 2-4 weeks, while complex enterprise applications can take 3-6 months. I provide detailed estimates after understanding requirements and always deliver quality over speed.

Do you work with startups?

Yes! I love working with startups. I can help validate your MVP, build scalable architecture, and provide technical guidance. I understand startup constraints and can deliver pragmatic solutions that balance speed and quality.
